Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

At NewsHub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our news aggregation services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Data

When you register for an account, we collect your name, email address, username, and encrypted password.

Usage Data

We collect information about which articles you read, save, and your preferred news categories to improve your personalized feed.

Device Data

We may collect information about your device, including IP address, browser type, and operating system for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Information

  • Personalization: To provide you with news that matches your interests.
  • Security: To protect your account and our platform from unauthorized access and fraud.
  • Analytics: To understand how our users interact with the site and improve the user experience.
  • Communication: To send you important updates about your account or changes to our terms.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to maintain your session, remember your theme preferences (Light/Dark mode), and track site performance via basic analytics tools.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption and salt-hashed passwords, to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Third Party Services

NewsHub integrates with third-party APIs (such as NewsAPI and GNews) to fetch content. These services may collect anonymous data about the requests. We also use FontAwesome and Google Fonts for our interface.

7. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your profile information and saved articles directly from your account settings.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
